This is a simple open-source application that takes an input of your dog's age in human years, and returns it in dog years.

The formula is obtained from a post by the American Kennel Club and can be found here: https://www.akc.org/expert-advice/lifestyle/did-you-know/how-to-calculate-dog-years-to-human-years/
